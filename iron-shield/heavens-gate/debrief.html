Overview

Heaven's Gate was the codename for the security patrol through the towns nearby to point talon. This patrol was successful at its primary aims, though there were secondary failures.

Command

  • Baseplate: Internet
  • Echo 1, Section Leader: Nudel
  • Echo 1, Fire Team Leader: Erik

Report

  • Echo 1 left Talon
  • Echo 1 entered La Riviere, made contact with local persons
  • Echo 1 moved toward St Martin, spotted an armed individual, no contact.
  • Echo 1 received fire. Fire effectively returned. 1 opfor killed, 2 bluefor injured.
  • Echo 1 noted Priest of St Martin evacuating.
  • Echo 1 found cache of weapons and explosives in St Martin. Cache safely detonated on-site.
    • Echo 3 was dispatched to ensure the area was secured and properly cleared.
  • Echo 1 continued to Cancon, recieved heavy and effective fire.
  • Echo 1 neutralised a number of opfor combatants.
    • St Martin's Priest was later found among the dead.
  • Echo 1 was informed that Echo 3 had lost communications at St Martin.
  • Echo 1 returned to St Matrin, finding that Echo 3 had been engaged, and bodies had been mined.
    • Echos 4 and 5 were dispatched for enhanced security and de-mining operations.
  • Echo 1 returned to Talon.

Objectives

  • Patrol La Riviere: Success.
  • Destroy St Martin Weapons Cache: Success
  • Patrol Cancon: Success

Secondary Objectives

  • Find Echo 3: Failed

Summary

Whilst the patrols themselves were successful, the loss of support personnel from a previously secured area shows the ability of the assigned groups. Information about the missing patrol has been forwarded to Nightshade, and will already be back in the US. We need to be prepared for other audacious moves, and also be ready for the US response.
