--- Operation Briefing ---

Overview

Threats have been made by an unknown group against the Arette Mobile Station, with the Gendarme and Government requesting the route and station be secured.

Execution

Echos 1 and 2 will convoy from Point Talon, passing the temporary Gendarme station, to the town of Arette. The Mobile Station, accompanied by a Tactical Team, will follow the convoy's route once cleared by the Platoon Leader.

Once the station is established, the Tactical Team will return to the temporary station.

Echos 1 and 2 will keep the station secure.

Once the minimum contact has been made between the Gendarme and locals, the station will pack up, and return to the temporary station, in convoy with Echos 1 and 2.

Once the mobile station is secure in the compound, all squads will RTB.

Available Assets

  • Approx 12x Motor Vehicles

Signal

  • Baseplate: Command
  • Echos 1 and 2: Infantry Sections
  • Goose: Gendarme Mobile Station
  • Gander: Gendarme Tactical Team

This mission will not have task notifications.
