--- INTELLIGENCE UPDATE, AS OF GOLDEN ANGEL ---

Faction Update, Defenders of Malden

Assets

The Defenders of Malden lost some supplies in a strongpoint after Operation Eagle's Reprieve, though a number of boxes were seen being removed from nearby bunkers.

Manpower

The Defenders lost 5 sections during the defence of their strongpoint, and 2 field officers. A number of personal escaped, including one who initiated a carbombing of Point Talon.

Faction Update, US

Assets

The USS Freedom, along with other support staff, have been removed from the area. Some US vehicles have been left behind to support further operations.

Update, Crime

A crackdown on crime was ordered by the Government, with the Gendarme increasing operations across the island, and the VCPs have been dismantled. The situation for individuals however, has not improved. However, we lack intelligence on criminal organisations on the island.
