--- Operation Briefing ---

Overview

A major operation on the island has disabled Malden's Air Base, our northern ourpost and a number of Gendarme. In the chaos caused by this, the pro-Government militia have taken the power plant in the city of Le Port, killing a number of Gendarme Security Forces and Plant Workers. Our response must be decisive; take back the power plant.

Execution

Time is of no object. Foxtrot 1 have the Northern Entry secure, and we have watercraft and surveillance assets in the area to prevent water egress. Clear the area, return it to government control. Do not forget your primary operational objectives.
