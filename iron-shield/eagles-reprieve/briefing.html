--- OPeration Briefing ---

Overview

Northwind have been made aware of a Defenders of Malden stronghold, where 2 US servicemen are held hostage. The US Armed Forces however, have been blocked from fielding further assets to launching an operation themselves due to Russian intervention in the UN.

Due to this, our objective is to secure the hostages and clear the outpost.

To compensate for the reduced manpower in the Sustainment Brigade, the US have moved the USS Freedom, a "Logistical Supply Vessel", closer to the shore.

Execution

Echo 1, Echo 2 and Ghost 1 will drive in convoy to a nearby point to the OPFOR position. Ghost 1 will move on foot to an overwatch position.

Echos 1 and 2 will move on foot into the compound, and search for the hostages.

Once found, Viking 1 will move into position, recieve the hostages, then exfiltrate.

Once the hostages are secure, Echos 1 and 2 will clear the compound, with Ghost 1 continuing to provide overwatch.

Once the compound is clear, all sections will RTB.
