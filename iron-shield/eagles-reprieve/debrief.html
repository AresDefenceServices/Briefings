Overview

Eagle's Reprieve was the codename for the hostage extraction from the Defenders of Malden stronghold. This patrol was successful at its primary aims, though there were secondary failures.

Command

  • Baseplate: Internet
  • Echo 1, Section Leader: Nudel
  • Ghost 1, Leader: Overlewd

Report

  • Echo 1 and Ghost 1 (Forces) left Talon
  • Forces moved to a staging area.
  • Ghost 1 moved to a position with oversight.
  • Echo 1 moved into the compound, clearing the main buildings.
  • Echo 1 cleared nearby bunkers.
  • Echo 1 found the hostages.
  • Viking 1 extracted the hostages.
  • Echo 1 and Ghost 1 regrouped.
  • Forces returned to base.
  • Spartan reported a carbomb at Point Talon.

Objectives

  • Extract the Hostages: Success.
  • Capture or Kill all onsite personal: Failed
  • Capture or Destroy all onsite material: Failed

Summary

Whilst the extraction was successful, there was much more equipment which could have been destroyed, and many persons evaded capture.
