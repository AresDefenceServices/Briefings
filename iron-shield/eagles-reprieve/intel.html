Faction Update, Defenders of Malden

Major Information

The Defenders of Malden have been confirmed as the group which attacked Echo 3 during Tasking Heavens Gate. They have contacted the Government of Malden, and have confirmed they are holding the two surviving members of Echo 3.

Assets

The Defenders of Malden lost a supply cache in the Saint Martin church.

Manpower

The Defenders lost 3 sections during the defence of Cancon, including a number of trained field commanders.

Local Support

The Defenders have lost the support of the populations of La Riviere and Cancon, through the murder of the local Priest.

Faction Update, IDAP

Manpower

IDAP have brought over a Construction Team (10 men) to assist in the rebuilding of the St Martin chapel.
They have also brought an additional water carrier.

Total manpower is now 30 men and 5 transport vehicles.

Faction Update, US

Assets

In response to the capture of Echo 3, the US has brought in the USS Freedom to provide air cover for hostage extraction missions.

Political Update, Russia

In response to events in Sahrani, Russia appears to be intent on opposing further UN intervention in Malden.

Assets Update

Close Air Support

Hunters 1 and 2 (A10-D) may be available for limited Close Air Support roles.

Viking 1 (UH-60) is available for transport and hostage extraction roles.
