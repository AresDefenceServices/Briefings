--- Operation Briefing ---

Overview

With the reclaimation of order, following Operation Shifting Tides, the pro-Government militia have gone to ground, and the anti-Government militia have not been observed for weeks. We have had limited SIGINT and HUMINT during this period. However, they are still planning their future operations, as an arms deal is taking place in the town of Arudy, as reported by a local.

Execution

Enter into the AO, and set up observation posts. Observe any deal which takes place. Follow any militia forces out of the area. Search for and destroy any illegal arms found.

Signal

  • Northwind: Command
  • Echo Actual: Platoon Section
  • Echos 1, 2 and 3: Infantry Sections
  • Redleg: 4 gun artillery battery
