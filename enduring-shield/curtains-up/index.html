--- OPERATION BRIEFING ---

Overview

Northwind has been provided maps of the local area, and has seen a number of areas of tactical importance which require security.

Our primary objective is to move to these zones, ensure they are secure, improve fortifications where possible, whilst improving rapport with local civilians.

Execution

Echos Actual, 1 through 3 will operate a combat patrol, either on foot or with motor vehicles, ensure the security of local zones, liaising with local forces and civilians along route.
